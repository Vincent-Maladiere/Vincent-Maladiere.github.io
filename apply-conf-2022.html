Here are some of my notes from the apply(conf) 2022!
For every presentation, I tried to write the most critical pieces of information that I would be able to remember afterwards; think of it as a low-pass filter of understanding. I also tried to include as many slides as I could find on the Slack of the event, please feel free to send me additional ones.
